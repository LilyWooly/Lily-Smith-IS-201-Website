Lily Smith

I'm Lily Smith, a current student at BYU. I'm just finishing up my third semester, and am working as a barista while I go to school.


Fun fact: I sang in a jazz choir in highschool, and recently got accepted into the jazz choir here at BYU!

A little about where I'm from Spokane, WA

Spokane is on the eastern side of Washington, near the panhandle of Idaho. I moved to Spokane in December of 2018 and have loved it ever since.


I attribute loving Spokane to my positive attitude towards to situation which let me just right into my new life. I developed this mindset after discover Stoicism - even when you can't control your situations, you can control your mindset. I highly recommend looking into it!
